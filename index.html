KoinKnight - Personal Assistant for Cryptocurrency Arbitrage

July 2017-September 2019

About the product : KoinKnight is a cryptocurrency arbitrage platform that helps users to identify arbitrage opportunities. It provides hyper-real time data with speed, accuracy and extensive combinations across coins, currency, exchanges and markets, resulting in 1 million+ arbitrage opportunities.
What I did : I worked as UI/UX Designer to design KoinKnight website and dashboard for complex data representation for arbitrage calculations.
Design Journey : The product has evolved and underwent iterations on the basis of user feedbacks and product roadmap. It started with creating a logo, visualising data in cards instead of tablular form, dashboard with new features and arbitrage tools, designing KoinKnight Widget for cyptocurrency blogs/websites, crafting a holistic look and feel for homepage and dashboard across devices .
KoinKnight Logo - food for thought! Total cryptocurrency market cap is $ 249 Trillion. It’s a vast ocean of arbitrage opportunities. Depth to the surface of this ocean is the volume of available arbitrage opportunities. Dark Blue color represents the deep ocean, Light Blue is the shallow waters. The opposite faced arrows represent the movement of cryptocurrencies. The gap between the arrows is the Arbitrage. The edges symbolizes the technological advantage/edge KoinKnight have with hyperreal time data capturing, processing and exhibit.

The problems with this tabular format were

1. Inability to scale wrt data

2. Unintuitive & not user friendly

3. Not mobile responsive

To solve these problems, a scalable and mobile responsive cards were designed which were easy to digest

I used color codes to show real-time updates in bid price, ask price and percentage of bid/ask order to be completed in an arbitrage.

We chose to arrange data based on how the user reads and what is more relevant in decision making process, so that it’s easy for anyone to relate the data logically.

Engage4more - Employee Engagement Activity Aggregrator

About the product : engage4more.com is an aggregator of employee engagement activities. The coporates can choose and book activities depending on their budget, their employee strength and the duration. The execution is done by 3rd party vendors.
What I did : I am currently working here as Product Lead and UI/UX Designer. The website has 200+ employee engagement ideas, with 150+ vendors aggregated Pan India.
Design Journey : engage4more.com transitioned from a cluttered corporate facing content based website to a clean UI highlighting the activity business as its core.
Website focuses on lead generation across its offerings. Integrated newsletter subscriptions with MailChimp to automate messaging. Integrated Push Notifications to fire updates to subscribers of any activity addition to the website. Integrated Google Tag Manager, user session recording through InspetLet and analytics across website. I’m currently working on integrating website with Zoho CRM to map lead journey.

Tikkle - B2B2C - Corporate byte sized learning App

About the product : Tikkle helps corporates to help their employees learn through byte sized learning modules, and keep them up-to-date through its communication channel. The corporates create learning module as per their requirements, upload them via Admin Panel. The module consists of episodes and quizzes, categorised into mandatory and non mandatory courses. Users can attempt the course anytime, and recieve a certification & credits on successful completion of the course.
What I did : I designed the Learn UI for Tikkle App. I designed the Learn Page which showcases the credits earned by the user, pending non-mandatory courses, mandatory courses and the progress status; the Course Catalouge page with categories, popular and recently added sections; module screens, quizzing screens, performance and feedback screens.
Design Journey : Tikkle introduced Learn feature to help corporates empower their employees. The byte sized learning modules helps employees learn anytime, anywhere. While designing UI, I visualised use cases for two users - Admin (Corporate HR/user uploading course content/module using admin panel) and employees (end user). For Admin, the ease of course/quiz CRUD, course tagging to employees/departments/locations and performance analysis were designed and developed in Admin Panel. For employees, the overall learning experience - the ease of navigating through the course modules and attempting quizzes and their performance analysis was our utmost priority. In order to improve the quality of content, I included a feedback mechanism at the end of every module. Tikkle is being used by more than 3000 employees across 3 corporates and is being used for content delivery during events and conferences.
