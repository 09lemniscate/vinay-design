KoinKnight - Personal Assistant for Cryptocurrency Arbitrage

July 2017-September 2019

About the product : KoinKnight is a cryptocurrency arbitrage platform that helps users to identify arbitrage opportunities. It provides hyper-real time data with speed, accuracy and extensive combinations across coins, currency, exchanges and markets, resulting in 1 million+ arbitrage opportunities.
What I did : I worked as UI/UX Designer to design KoinKnight website and dashboard for complex data representation for arbitrage calculations.
Design Journey : The product has evolved and underwent iterations on the basis of user feedbacks and product roadmap. It started with creating a logo, visualising data in cards instead of tablular form, dashboard with new features and arbitrage tools, designing KoinKnight Widget for cyptocurrency blogs/websites, crafting a holistic look and feel for homepage and dashboard across devices .

Engage4more - Employee Engagement Activity Aggregrator

July 2017-September 2019

Objective: engage4more.com is an aggregator platform for employee engagement activities. The corporates can book activities depending on their budget, their employee strength and the duration. The execution is done by 3rd party vendors.
What I did : I worked here as Product Lead and UI/UX Designer.
Problem to be solved:
Keeping corporates clients (especially CXOs, HR and Admin as our target audiences) at the centre, we identified the problem areas by user calling and interviews
1. Discovery
2. Trust
3. Transparency
Solution:
By website re-design, we solved these problems by showcasing a list of activities, establishing trust by highlighting the work company did in the field of employee engagement via client testimonials, range of clients they managed. As highlighted during user research, cost of the activity and the number of people that can be engaged in a single session helped us solve the issue of transparency.
The scalable acitvity card focused on highlighting the decision enabling parameters like images, video, price, discounts, number of people. The images were re-designed to incorporate real people in offices performing said activties. Showing the explored variations of the activity card, last option is the final design that we developed.
Events page was designed to convey the story of grandeur events done for corporate clients, and generate leads for the events vertical of engage4more. The corporates look for relevant themes, diverse ideas and most importantly seamless execution stories while discovery

Tikkle - B2B2C - Corporate byte sized learning App

July 2017-September 2019

About the product : Tikkle helps corporates to help their employees learn through byte sized learning modules, and keep them up-to-date through its communication channel.
The corporates create learning module as per their requirements, upload them through their Admin Panel. The module consists of episodes and quiz, categorised into mandatory and non mandatory courses. Users can attempt the course and recieve a certification & credits on successful completion of the course.
What I did : I designed the gamified Learn feature of Tikkle App for Android & iOS.
Design Journey : The byte sized learning modules helps employees learn anytime, anywhere. While designing UI, I had to visualise use cases for mandatory and non-mandatory courses. The module card incorporated credits for the module, image, time of module upload, title of the module and ratings. The progress bar inside module helps user to keep track of their progress. On successful completion, the user is able to see their performance
